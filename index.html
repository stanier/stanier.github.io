about me


Student. Junior developer. Admirer of Doorknobs. H2G2 Enthusiast.

When I was younger, I struggled in socializing with many of my classmates. I eventually took up gaming as a hobby and immersed myself within video games in my spare time. I used video games as a way to escape the worries of the world, to enjoy myself and left all my worries be null. More and more people started getting involved in video games as time went on, and the gamer community became rather tainted with hatred.

Realizing that video games were no longer what they used to be, I began to look for a new hobby. That's when I got into programming. I began by teaching myself BASIC and later went on to more advanced languages and concepts in the category of computer sciences. I am self-taught in Java, C#, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Bash, Batch, Lua, Coffeescript and PHP. I am also taking classes for C++ and VB .NET. I also have experience with microcontrollers and hobbyist electronics, as well as single board computers and industrial servers.

While my funds may be limited as a student, I still have plans to continue my projects and experiences.
